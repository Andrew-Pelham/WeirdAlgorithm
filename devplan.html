Platforms

In terms of platforms, we plan to primarily use PHP for back end development, and HTML for the front end, with MySQL powering the database.

Data Source

Our data is sourced from the Carnegia Mellon Pronouncing Dictionary, a dictionary with over 134,000 words and their pronunications and syllables. The words are based on the ARPAbet standards for pronunciation.
